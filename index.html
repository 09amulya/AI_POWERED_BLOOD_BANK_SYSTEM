How It Works

Three seamless steps from emergency request to life-saving donation — powered by smart blood group matching.

01
📝
Register

Donors sign up with health info, blood group, and location. Our system computes eligibility instantly using rule-based logic — soon powered by AI.

02
🚨
Request

Hospitals or patients submit blood requests with patient details, units required, and urgency level — from normal to critical emergencies.

03
🔗
Match

The matching engine instantly finds compatible, eligible donors based on blood group compatibility and donation eligibility rules.

Features

Everything You Need

A complete system built for real emergencies, with an architecture ready for AI integration.

👤
Donor Registration & Profiles

Full donor profiles with health info, eligibility tracking, and donation history. Eligibility auto-calculated on registration.

🩸
Blood Group Compatibility Engine

Built-in compatibility chart ensures O- donors match O- requests, AB+ accepts all groups — medically accurate matching.

⚡
Emergency Priority Queue

Critical, urgent, and normal urgency levels ensure life-threatening cases are prioritized. Donors are ranked by match score.

🔔
Instant Donor Notifications

Matched donors are notified immediately with patient details, hospital location, and urgency level so they can respond fast.

📊
Admin Dashboard

Real-time stats: total donors, eligible donors, pending requests, successful matches, and blood group breakdown.

🔄
Eligibility Auto-Checker

Evaluates age, weight, last donation date, and health conditions automatically to determine if a donor is ready to give blood.
